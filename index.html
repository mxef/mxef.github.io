Michalis Xefteris

I am a second year PhD student in Computer Science at LIP6, Sorbonne Université working with Evripidis Bampis and Bruno Escoffier. I completed my undergraduate studies in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering of the National Technical University of Athens, where I was advised by Dimitris Fotakis.

My research focuses on the design and theoretical analysis of online and learning-augmented algorithms.
