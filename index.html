Michalis Xefteris

I am a postdoctoral researcher in Computer Science at Universität Bremen as an Alexander von Humboldt Fellow, hosted by Nicole Megow. Prior to that, I was a postdoc at the Athens University of Economics and Business, hosted by Vangelis Markakis. I received my PhD in Computer Science in 2024 from Sorbonne Université, under the supervision of Evripidis Bampis and Bruno Escoffier.

My research focuses on the design and theoretical analysis of online, approximation and learning-augmented algorithms.
